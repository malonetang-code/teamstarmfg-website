About Us

群新工业

Wei Qun Cutting Tools Group

群新工业股份有限公司在台湾已有40多年的历史，是专业从事生产各项产业之工业刀具的公司。1990年在深圳创立伟群精密机械制品有限公司，在中国也有30多年的生产经验。公司集研发、自主设计、制造于一体，能够满足新产品的设计、试做及产品验证。

Wei Qun Cutting Tools Industrial Co., Ltd. has over 40 years of history in Taiwan as a professional manufacturer of industrial cutting tools. Established Shenzhen Wei Qun Precision Machinery in 1990, with over 30 years of production experience in China. Integrating R&D, design, and manufacturing to meet new product development, prototyping, and validation needs.

公司一直秉持着"持续提升品质，满足客户需求"的理念来永续经营，致力于成为国内外客户最信赖的工业刀具伙伴。

The company upholds the philosophy of "continuous quality improvement and meeting customer needs" for sustainable operations, committed to being the most trusted industrial cutting tool partner for customers worldwide.
